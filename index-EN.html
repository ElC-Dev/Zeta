Zeta Translations

Zeta Translations offers high quality, speedy translations, and great prices! Our services include free, or basic translation, website translation, proofreading, subtitling, copywriting translation, as well as the production of copywriting text in any of our supported languages Our translations take into account all sorts of idiomatic expressions, and nuances, while also preserving the essence and original meaning of your text!

Our Services

Free Translation

Translation of all sorts of texts, including curriculum, articles, schoolwork, and the like.

Website Translation

Translation of all your website text

Proofreading

Proofreading of all sorts of texts, including monographies, articles, schoolwork, and the like.

Subtitling

Translation of subtitle text, as well as audio transcription and subtitling of media files.

Available languages: English, Japanese, and Portuguese
